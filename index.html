Summary

Content

'Cosmic Seeker' website is designed to satisfy your curiosity about the fascinating world of science. Here, you'll have the opportunity to delve into the profound mysteries of Space Science, navigating through NASA's incredible databases of galaxies, planets, and stars. Explore the boundless universe and gain new insights through its stunning images and videos. Furthermore, our website shifts its focus to Earth, covering critical aspects of Biology. Here you can Biodiversity and Botany: Explore the vast distribution of life on Earth, diverse animal and plant species, and their habitats through the GBIF (Global Biodiversity Information Facility) API. Discover the astonishing diversity of life on our planet using information collected by scientists worldwide. 'Cosmic Seeker' provides you with the knowledge and visual experiences you seek, whether you're learning about the immensity of the universe, exploring the hidden life in the depths of the ocean, or uncovering the diversity of life on Earth. It will serve as an essential resource for science enthusiasts, students, and anyone with a thirst for knowledge.
